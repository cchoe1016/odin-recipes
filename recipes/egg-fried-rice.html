SIMPLE Egg Fried Rice Recipe


Egg fried rice is a simple, yet delicious Chinese fried rice dish. Anyone can easily make it at home.
Of course you can add other ingredients to the fried rice, but today we will keep it classic!
You will find this egg fried rice dish way better than takeout!



Ingredients

(Serves 3)

  • 2 cups of white rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 stalk of green onion
  • 3 T of cooking oil
  • 1 teaspoon of chicken stock
  • 1/2 T of salt
  • 1/2 T of sesame oil
  • A bit of black pepper

Steps to make this EASY Egg Fried Rice

  1. Mix the eggs well and slice the green onion stalk
  2. Add the oil to the frying pan (or wok) and scramble the eggs
  3. When the egg is about 80% cooked, mix the rice well so that there are no large clumps of rice
  4. Add the sliced green onion and stir fry with the rice and eggs until everything is cookied
  5. Add in the chicken stock powder, salt, and pepper.
  6. Drizzle some sesmae oil and stir fry one last time before serving!

**It is best to use cooled rice instead of hot rice for this dish
**You can use vegetable stock powder if you do not have chicken stock powder
