Easy Braised Tofu Recipe

Braised Tofu is known as Dubu Jorim in Korean.
It is a simple braised dish you can make using simple sauce.




Ingredients


  • 1 pack of firm tofu
  • half stalk of green onion
  • 1 onion
  • 1 red pepper
  • 1 cup of chicken stock
  • sesame seeds


Ingredients for Simple Sauce

  • 3 T soy sauce
  • 2 T rice wine
  • 1 T red pepper flakes
  • 1 T sugar
  • 1/2 T minced garlic
  • 1 T Sesame oil
  • black pepper (a bit)


Steps to the Recipe



  1. Slice the firm tofu and sprinkle some salt and pepper. Let it sit for 10 minutes.
  2. Slice the red pepper, green onion, and onion
  3. In a separate bowl, mix all the ingredients from the "Ingredients for Simple Sauce".
  4. Pour some oil and pan fry the tofu until golden brown.
  5. After taking out the tofu, place the sliced onions on the bottom of the pan and add the chicken stock.
  6. Place the pan fried tofu on top of the onions and drizzle the simple suace on the tofu.
  7. After cooking the braised tofu for 5 minutes, add the green onions and red pepper. Let it cook for another minute or two.
  8. Sprinkle some sesame seeds on the braised tofu and enjoy with rice!
